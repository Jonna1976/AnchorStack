# AnchorStack

A methodology for building things where being wrong has consequences.

AnchorStack makes anchoring part of how you build - every commit, decision,
and output on an independent timeline. Not as a constraint. As a compass.

---

## The problem it solves

Prompts are stateless. Every AI session starts from zero. The decisions you
made last week, the boundaries you validated last month - none of it travels
with the output.

AnchorStack encodes what your product means, what it promises, and what it
cannot say - and carries that across every session, every domain, every output.

---

## Three skills. One workflow.

**1. `anchor-guardian`** - Load first. Always.
Your north star. Encodes what your product stands for - starting with your
north star question, the one boundary that overrides everything.

**2. `anchor-domain`** - One per area of your product.
Structured knowledge for architecture, legal, GTM, investors, partners, design.
Decisions already made, so they travel with every output.

**3. `anchor-review`** - Run before anything leaves.
Verifies any output against all active anchors. Not because you doubt the
output. Because anchored outputs build trust over time.

---

## Getting started

```
anchor-guardian  ->  define your north star question
anchor-domain    ->  encode your first domain
anchor-review    ->  verify your first output
```
