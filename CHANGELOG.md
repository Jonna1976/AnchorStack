## [0.1.0] - March 2026

First public release. Built on 14 months of internal use at [Umarise](https://umarise.com).

### Added

- **anchor-guardian** - constitutional layer with north star question
- **anchor-domain** - structured knowledge template, one per product area
- **anchor-review** - binary verification against all active anchors
- **QUICKSTART.md** - install in 30 seconds for Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, Claude.ai
- **ETHOS.md** - why this exists
- Documentation: [guardian](docs/guardian.md), [skill writing](docs/skills.md), [checklist pattern](docs/checklist.md)
- Examples: `product-integrity/` (13 domains, 14 months), `legal-tech/` (minimal starter)
