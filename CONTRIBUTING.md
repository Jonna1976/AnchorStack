# Contributing

AnchorStack is a methodology. Contributions that improve the methodology are welcome.

---

## What belongs here

**Examples** - If you implemented AnchorStack for a domain (medical, financial, compliance,
identity, content authenticity) and are willing to share the structure. Not the content -
the structure. Your constraints are yours. The pattern is what helps others.

**Improved templates** - Structural improvements to the guardian, domain, or review
templates. Based on real use, not theory.

**Failure modes** - Patterns of drift that the current templates do not address.
The methodology improves by encountering real failure modes.

**Documentation** - Better explanations, more examples, translations.

---

## What belongs in your own repo

Your guardian. Your domain skills. Your constraints, validated formulations, and
forbidden language lists. Those are specific to your product.

Reference AnchorStack as the methodology. Link here. But your content stays with
your product.

---

## The one rule

If you propose a change, explain what failure mode it addresses.

"This is better" is not sufficient.
"This prevents X, which I encountered when Y" is.

---

## Process

Fork, branch, PR. For significant changes, open an issue first.
