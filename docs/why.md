# Why I built this

*by Jonna Fassbender*

---

I am not a developer.

I am 49 years old. I built this alone, with AI as my team.
No investors. No co-founder. No engineering background.

I built it because I believe the world needs it.
Not because I want recognition. I have been there. That is not what drives this.

---

## The problem I could not ignore

AI is growing faster than our ability to trust it.

Not because AI is untrustworthy. Because we have no independent way to verify
what it produced, when, and under what circumstances.

Every system that records its own history can rewrite it.
Every platform that stores your outputs controls what those outputs prove.
Every AI agent that acts on your behalf leaves traces only inside systems
that someone else controls.

We are building on sand. And most people do not see it yet.

---

## What anchoring is

An anchor is independent proof.

Not a log in your own system. Not a backup you control.
A cryptographic fingerprint of something that existed at a specific moment,
recorded on a timeline nobody controls. Bitcoin. Verifiable by anyone. Forever.

You do not anchor because something will go wrong.
You anchor because the world deserves proof that is not self-serving.

A doctor who keeps records. An architect who archives drawings.
A builder who signs their work.

Not out of fear. Out of integrity.

---

## Why I am pro-AI and pro-anchoring

I use Claude every day. AI has made this possible - this product, this methodology,
this document. I would not be here without it.

But I watch the growth and I see the gap.

AI agents are becoming autonomous. They make decisions. They produce outputs.
They act in the world on our behalf. And when they do, there is no independent
record that proves what happened, when, and why.

We need that record. Not to limit AI. To trust it.

Anchoring is not a constraint on AI. It is the foundation that makes AI trustworthy.
The difference between "the system says so" and "here is proof that exists outside
any system."

That is the gap I am building to fill.

---

## What I want

I want anchoring to be the default.

Like `git commit`. Like version control. Like SSL.
Something that happens automatically, that nobody thinks about,
that simply exists as part of how we build.

Not because someone mandated it.
Because builders understand that their work deserves an independent record.
Because AI systems deserve to be trusted - and trust requires proof.
Because the humans who come after us deserve to know what was real.

---

## Who I am

I am a founder. I run [Umarise](https://umarise.com) - the infrastructure
that makes anchoring possible at scale.

I built AnchorStack because the methodology should be free.
The infrastructure is the business. The methodology is the gift.

I built it in silence, over the past months, because the problem demanded it.
I am publishing it now because it is ready - and because the moment is now.

AI is not slowing down. The gap between what AI produces and what we can
independently verify is growing every day.

Anchoring is how we keep up.
